# Community & members

NoBadFonts is more than a font library—it's a community of designers who care about typography quality. Members can upload fonts, build profiles, and connect with other type designers.

## User roles

The platform has three user roles with different capabilities:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="User" icon="user">
    Basic account with browsing and download access
  </Card>
  <Card title="Member" icon="shield-check">
    Approved contributor who can upload fonts
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="crown">
    Platform moderator with curation rights
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### User

Anyone who creates an account starts as a **User**. Users can:

- ✅ Browse the full font catalog
- ✅ Download fonts for personal use
- ✅ Use the font tester and pairing tools
- ✅ Favorite fonts and track downloads
- ✅ Apply for membership
- ❌ Upload fonts
- ❌ Edit font metadata

### Member

**Members** are verified contributors who have been approved to upload fonts. Members can:

- ✅ Everything a User can do, plus:
- ✅ Upload new fonts to the catalog
- ✅ Manage their uploaded fonts
- ✅ Add banner galleries to font pages
- ✅ Update font metadata and tags
- ✅ Appear in the members directory
- ✅ Have a public profile page
- ❌ Modify other members' fonts
- ❌ Access admin tools

<Note>
To become a Member, you must apply and be approved by an admin. This ensures font quality and prevents spam.
</Note>

### Admin

**Admins** are platform moderators who curate the catalog. Admins can:

- ✅ Everything a Member can do, plus:
- ✅ Approve or reject membership applications
- ✅ Edit any font's metadata
- ✅ Mark fonts as featured, trending, or editor's picks
- ✅ Publish or unpublish fonts
- ✅ Access the admin dashboard
- ✅ Promote users to member status
- ✅ Run database maintenance tools

## Membership application

To upload fonts, you need to apply for membership:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create an account">
    Sign up with your email or Google account at [nobadfonts.in/auth](https://nobadfonts.in/auth).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add your profile information:
    - Full name
    - Username
    - Bio
    - Profile picture
    - Portfolio links (Behance, Dribbble, Instagram, etc.)

    A complete profile helps admins understand your design background.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply for membership">
    Navigate to your profile page and click **Apply for Membership**.

    Your application will be reviewed by an admin, typically within 2-3 business days.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Membership status">
    Your profile will show one of four membership statuses:

    <AccordionGroup>
      <Accordion title="None">
        You haven't applied for membership yet. Continue using the platform as a User.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Pending">
        Your application is under review by an admin. You'll receive an email when a decision is made.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Approved">
        Congratulations! Your role has been upgraded to **Member**. You can now upload fonts.
      </Accordion>

      <Accordion title="Rejected">
        Your application was not approved at this time. Admins may provide feedback. You can reapply after improving your portfolio or profile.
      </Accordion>
    </AccordionGroup>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
To increase your chances of approval, showcase existing type design work in your portfolio links and write a thoughtful bio explaining your interest in typography.
</Tip>

## Member directory

The [Members page](https://nobadfonts.in/members) showcases all approved contributors:

**Features:**
- Grid view of member profiles with avatars
- Links to each member's portfolio and social profiles
- Filter by activity or alphabetically
- View fonts uploaded by each member

**Member profile pages** (`/members/:id`) show:
- Full bio and contact information
- Portfolio and social links (Behance, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn)
- All fonts uploaded by that member
- Contributor statistics (total fonts, total downloads)

## Designer pages

Each font designer has a dedicated page at `/designers/:designerName`:

- **Fonts by designer** - View all fonts from a specific designer
- **Designer bio** - Learn about the type designer's background
- **Links** - Visit the designer's portfolio or website

<Note>
Designer pages are automatically created when fonts are uploaded. The designer name comes from the font metadata.
</Note>

## Community guidelines

To maintain quality and respect, all members must follow these guidelines:

### Font uploads

- **Quality first** - Only upload production-ready fonts with complete character sets
- **Proper licensing** - Ensure you have rights to distribute the font
- **Accurate metadata** - Provide correct designer names, categories, and tags
- **No duplicates** - Check if a font already exists before uploading

### Respectful conduct

- **Be professional** - Treat other members with respect
- **Give credit** - Always attribute the original designer
- **No spam** - Don't upload low-quality or test fonts
- **Report issues** - Flag problematic content to admins

<Warning>
Violating community guidelines may result in membership revocation and account suspension.
</Warning>

## Profile customization

Members can customize their public profiles:

**Basic information:**
- Full name
- Username (appears in URLs)
- Email (private, only for notifications)
- Bio (Markdown supported)
- Avatar image

**Social links:**
- Website
- Behance
- Twitter / X
- Instagram
- LinkedIn
- PayPal.me (for donations)

**Privacy:**
- All profile fields except email are publicly visible
- Users can update their profile anytime via the `/profile` page

## Statistics & analytics

Member profiles display contributor statistics:

- **Total fonts uploaded**
- **Total downloads across all fonts**
- **Favorite count** (total favorites on your fonts)
- **Recent activity** (uploads, updates)

<Tip>
These metrics help designers showcase their impact on the community.
</Tip>

## Admin dashboard

Admins have access to a dashboard at `/admin` with tools for:

**User management:**
- View all users and their roles
- Approve or reject membership applications
- Promote users to member status
- View pending applications

**Font curation:**
- Mark fonts as featured, trending, or editor's picks
- Publish or unpublish fonts
- Run WOFF2 scanner to fix missing file URLs
- Bulk edit metadata

**Platform statistics:**
- Total users, members, and fonts
- Recent uploads and downloads
- Most popular fonts this week

<Note>
Admin access is granted manually by existing admins. Contact the project maintainer if you're interested in helping moderate the platform.
</Note>
