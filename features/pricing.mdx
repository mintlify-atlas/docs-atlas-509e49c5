NoBadFonts offers straightforward licensing options for both personal and commercial projects. All fonts in the catalog are available under clear terms with no hidden fees.

## How licensing works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download a font">
    Browse the [font catalog](/features/font-catalog) and download any font for free to evaluate it in your design.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use for personal projects">
    Fonts downloaded without a commercial license are automatically covered under the Free plan for personal use.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upgrade for commercial use">
    When you're ready to use a font in a commercial project, purchase the Pro license for that specific font family.

    <Note>
    **Coming soon**: Integrated checkout will be available directly on font detail pages. Currently, contact the designer for commercial licensing.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive license key">
    After purchase, you'll receive:
    - License certificate with purchase details
    - Commercial license key
    - Receipt for your records
    - Access to premium support
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a separate license for web and desktop use?">
    No. Both Free and Pro licenses cover all use cases within their respective terms. A single Pro license allows you to use the font on websites, in apps, and in print materials.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use the same font for multiple clients?">
    Yes, with a Pro license. Once you purchase commercial rights, you can use the font for unlimited client projects without additional fees.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my team grows beyond 3 users?">
    The Pro license covers up to 3 designers using the font simultaneously. If your team expands, you can purchase additional seats or contact the designer for volume licensing options.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I redistribute or resell fonts?">
    No. Licenses grant usage rights only. You cannot:
    - Redistribute font files to third parties
    - Resell or sublicense fonts
    - Share licenses with non-team members
    - Include fonts in themes/templates for resale
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there refunds?">
    Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis by the original designer. Generally, refunds are available within 7 days if the font has a technical issue or doesn't match the description.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What license types are available?">
    Fonts on NoBadFonts use various open-source and commercial licenses:
    - **OFL (Open Font License)** - Free for personal and commercial use with attribution
    - **MIT License** - Permissive open-source license
    - **Apache 2.0** - Enterprise-friendly open-source license
    - **Commercial** - Proprietary license requiring purchase

    Check each font's detail page for its specific license type.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## License compliance

### Embedding fonts on the web

When embedding fonts with `@font-face`, ensure compliance:

```html
<!-- ✅ Correct: Host fonts on your own domain -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="/fonts/fonts.css">
```

```html
<!-- ⚠️ CDN embedding: Allowed for Pro licenses -->
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://fonts.nobadfonts.in/css?family=Inter">
```

<Warning>
Do not hotlink directly to font files from the NoBadFonts CDN in production. Download and self-host for better performance and license compliance.
</Warning>

### Attribution requirements

Some fonts (particularly OFL-licensed fonts) require attribution:

```html
<!-- Example attribution in your website footer -->
<footer>
  <p>Typography: <a href="https://nobadfonts.in/fonts/inter">Inter</a> by Rasmus Andersson</p>
</footer>
```

Check the font's license details page for specific attribution requirements.
